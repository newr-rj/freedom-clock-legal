Privacy Policy for FreedomClock

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Introduction

FreedomClock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Retirement Planning Data: Age, income, savings, investment information, and retirement goals you enter to calculate your retirement timeline
  • Name: Optional name for personalization of the AI assistant
  • Location Preferences: Country, state, and city selections for cost of living calculations

Automatically Collected Information

  • Crash Data: Technical information about app crashes to help us improve stability (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics to optimize user experience
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous interaction data to understand how features are used

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Calculate and display your personalized retirement timeline
  • Provide AI-powered retirement planning assistance (only with your explicit consent for external AI services)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Enhance features based on usage patterns

Data Sharing with AI Services (Optional)

By default, no retirement planning data is shared with external services. If you choose to enable the "Use external AI for answers" feature in Settings:

  • What We Share: Your retirement plan inputs (age, current savings, income, monthly spending, retirement location preferences) and your questions to the AI assistant
  • Why We Share: To provide personalized, context-aware retirement planning advice and answer your specific questions
  • Who We Share With: Perplexity AI and Groq AI services for natural language processing and response generation
  • Your Control: You can enable or disable this feature at any time in Settings. Disabling it stops all data sharing immediately
  • Consent Required: You must explicitly consent before any data is shared. The app will not share data without your permission

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your retirement planning data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest using iOS data protection
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Anonymous Analytics: Analytics data is collected anonymously and cannot identify you personally

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For crash reporting, performance monitoring, and authentication
  • RevenueCat/StoreKit: For processing premium subscriptions (payment processing handled by Apple)
  • AI Services (Perplexity/Groq): For the AI retirement assistant. When you enable "Use external AI for answers" in Settings (with your explicit consent), your retirement plan inputs (age, savings, income, spending, location) and questions are sent to these AI services to generate personalized responses. These services do not retain your data after processing your queries. You can disable this feature at any time in Settings.

These services have their own privacy policies and data practices. You must provide explicit consent before any data is shared with external AI services.

Your Choices

  • Data Deletion: You can delete all your data by using "Reset Plan" in Settings or by uninstalling the app
  • AI Data Sharing: Control whether your data is shared with external AI services via the "Use external AI for answers" toggle in Settings
  • Analytics Opt-Out: You can limit analytics collection through your device's privacy settings
  • Subscription Management: Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings
  • Revoke AI Consent: Turn off the AI toggle in Settings to immediately stop sharing data with external AI services

Children's Privacy

FreedomClock is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date.

California Residents

International Users

If you are accessing FreedomClock from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
