Privacy Policy for FreedomClock

Last Updated: January 19, 2026

Introduction

FreedomClock ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Scope and Application

This Privacy Policy applies to the FreedomClock mobile application for iOS, including all features and functionality. This includes the optional AI assistant feature that, when enabled by you, shares your user-entered data with third-party AI service providers (Perplexity AI and Groq) to generate personalized retirement planning responses.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Retirement Planning Data: Age, income, savings, investment information, and retirement goals you enter to calculate your retirement timeline
  • Name: Optional name for personalization of the AI assistant
  • Location Preferences: Country, state, and city selections for cost of living calculations

Automatically Collected Information

  • Crash Data: Technical information about app crashes to help us improve stability (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics to optimize user experience
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous interaction data to understand how features are used

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Retirement Calculations: To run retirement timeline calculations and simulations based on your inputs
  • AI Assistance (Optional): To generate AI-powered responses when you enable external AI services (requires explicit consent)
  • App Functionality: To provide core features, store your preferences, and deliver the services you request
  • Performance & Debugging: To monitor app performance, diagnose crashes, and fix technical issues via Firebase Crashlytics
  • App Improvement: To analyze anonymous usage patterns and improve features

Data Sharing with External AI Services (Optional)

By default, no retirement planning data is shared with external services. All calculations run locally on your device. If you choose to enable the "Use external AI for answers" toggle in Settings, the following applies:

What Data Is Shared

When external AI is enabled, we share only the information necessary to answer your questions:

  • Your Question Text: The exact text of questions you ask the AI assistant
  • Retirement Plan Inputs: Specific examples include:
    • Your current age and retirement age target
    • Current savings amount
    • Monthly income and monthly spending amounts
    • Retirement location preferences (country, state, city selections)
    • Investment allocation percentages
  • Minimal Conversation Context: Recent messages in your current chat session to maintain conversational flow

What We Do NOT Share

  • Personal Identifiers: We do NOT send your name, email address, phone number, or device identifiers unless you explicitly include them in your question text
  • Account Information: No Apple ID or subscription details are shared
  • App Usage Analytics: Your broader app usage patterns are not sent to AI providers

Who We Share With & Their Practices

  • Service Providers: Currently Perplexity AI and Groq (using Llama models) for natural language processing and response generation
  • Their Data Practices: These services process the data you send to generate responses. Their data retention and usage practices are governed by their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies:
    • Perplexity AI Privacy Policy: https://www.perplexity.ai/privacy
    • Groq Privacy Policy: https://groq.com/privacy-policy/
  • No Guarantees: We cannot guarantee or verify the data retention practices of third-party AI providers. Use external AI services at your own discretion

Your Control

  • Opt-In Required: You must explicitly consent and enable the feature before any data is shared
  • Easy Toggle: Turn external AI on or off at any time in Settings → AI Assistant
  • Immediate Effect: Disabling the toggle immediately stops all data sharing with external AI services

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your retirement planning data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest using iOS data protection
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Anonymous Analytics: Analytics data is collected anonymously and cannot identify you personally

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services to provide app functionality:

  • Firebase (Google): For crash reporting, performance monitoring, and authentication. Firebase collects technical and diagnostic data. Learn more: Firebase Privacy
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing premium subscriptions. Payment processing is handled by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • External AI Services (Optional - Perplexity/Groq): Only used when you enable "Use external AI for answers" in Settings. See "Data Sharing with External AI Services" section above for full details on what is shared and how to control it

Important: Third-party services operate under their own privacy policies and terms of service. We do not control how they handle data and cannot guarantee their practices. You must provide explicit consent before any retirement planning data is shared with external AI services.

Data Retention

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • What: All your retirement planning inputs, calculation history, preferences, and AI chat history
  • How Long: Data persists on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Deletion: Use "Reset Plan" in Settings to delete all local data, or uninstall the app

Data Stored on Our Servers

  • What: We do NOT store your retirement planning data on our servers. All calculations and data storage happen locally on your device
  • Analytics & Crashes: Anonymous crash reports and performance metrics are retained by Firebase per their retention policies (typically 90 days for crashes, aggregated analytics may be retained longer)

External AI Service Data

  • Our Logs: We do NOT log or store the questions and data you send to external AI services
  • AI Provider Retention: Data sent to Perplexity and Groq is subject to their own retention policies. We cannot control or verify how long they retain your data. Consult their privacy policies for details

Your Choices & Controls

You have the following options to control your data:

AI Data Sharing Control

  • Enable/Disable External AI: Go to Settings → AI Assistant and toggle "Use external AI for answers" on or off at any time
  • Default Setting: External AI is OFF by default; you must opt in to use it
  • Immediate Effect: Turning off the toggle immediately stops all data sharing with AI providers

Data Deletion

  • Delete Plan Data: In Settings, tap "Reset Plan" to permanently delete all your inputs and calculation history
  • Delete AI Chat History: In the AI Assistant screen, tap the trash icon to clear your conversation history
  • Uninstall App: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data from your device

Other Controls

  • Analytics: Limit analytics collection through your device's privacy settings (Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements)
  • Subscription Management: Manage or cancel premium subscriptions through your Apple ID account settings
  • Questions or Requests: Contact us at support@newronrobotics.com for data-related questions or requests

Children's Privacy

FreedomClock is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
